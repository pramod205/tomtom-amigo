How to Update TomTom AmiGO Maps

TomTom AmiGO is a free navigation app offering real-time traffic updates and speed camera alerts. To ensure the most accurate routes and the latest map data, it's essential to keep the app updated regularly. Here's how you can do it easily.

Why Update TomTom AmiGO?

  • Get the latest road changes and routes
  • Improve navigation accuracy and rerouting
  • Receive updated speed camera alerts and zones
  • Enhance app performance and stability

Steps to Update TomTom AmiGO (Android & iOS)

  • Open the Google Play Store or Apple App Store
  • Search for TomTom AmiGO
  • If an update is available, tap Update
  • Wait for the update to complete, then open the app
  • Make sure you have internet access for the latest data

Enable Auto-Update (Recommended)

To ensure you always have the latest AmiGO maps and features:

  • Go to your device's App Store settings
  • Enable auto-update for installed apps
  • This way, TomTom AmiGO will update automatically in the background

Tips for Better Performance

  • Clear cache periodically from app settings
  • Enable GPS and location accuracy
  • Keep mobile data or Wi-Fi turned on during travel
  • Close other heavy apps running in the background

By keeping your TomTom AmiGO app updated, you ensure a seamless and safe driving experience with real-time insights and efficient navigation on every route.
